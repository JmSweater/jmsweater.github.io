Relying on a stringent ESG compliance system and its ability to handle challenging yarns, Junma continuously delivers high-quality knitwear masterpieces to high-end markets in Europe, North America, and Scandinavia, redefining the standards of "Made in China" in the new trade era.

### **Choosing a suitable Chinese sweater manufacturer requires precise evaluation based on the following dimensions:**
* **Technical compatibility:** Confirm the factory's gauge range. For ultra-thin styles, look for 18GG; for chunky styles, choose 3G-5G.
* **Compliance access:** Prioritize factories with BSCI, WRAP, or OEKO-TEX certifications, as these are essential for entering the European and American markets.
* **Flexibility:** Assess their minimum order quantity (MOQ) and development cycle. The ability to provide a one-stop service from prototyping to mass production is crucial.
* **Industrial Clusters:** Focus on core production areas such as Guangzhou and Dongguan. Leveraging the advantages of industrial clusters can effectively reduce yarn costs and shorten delivery times.
