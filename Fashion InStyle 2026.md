## 🛠 Core Manufacturing Logic

We treat knitwear as a technical discipline. By applying a Computer Science background to textile physics, we have optimized our production stack for high-precision delivery and modular scalability.

### 1. Technical Specifications & Hardware
* **Precision Gauging:** Specialized in **18GG (Fine Gauge)** and **4GG (Heavy Gauge)** manufacturing.
* **Physical Trait Analysis:** Advanced patterning systems analyze yarn elasticity, tension, and recovery metrics before production.
* **Knitting Tech:** Fully fashioned technology ensuring ergonomic fits and **Zero-Waste** output.
* **Production Capacity:** A 3,000 sqm facility in Guangzhou equipped with **110 automated high-precision knitting machines**.

### 2. Material Stack
* **`recycled-cashmere.grs`**: GRS-certified circular materials delivering premium haptics without environmental compromise.
* **`merino-washable.v2026`**: Performance-driven, machine-washable Merino wool tailored for the 2026 urban commuter market.
* **Circular Fibers:** Integration of bio-based and recycled yarns into high-end fashion architectures.
